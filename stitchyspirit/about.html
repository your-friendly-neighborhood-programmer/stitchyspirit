High Quality, Handmade, Custom Crocheted Products

Stitchy Spirit is a small business that specializes in creating custom crocheted products. We offer a wide variety of items, including stuffed animals, blankets, hats, scarves, and more. Our products are made with high-quality materials and are designed to last.

Whether you're looking for a unique gift or just want to treat yourself, Stitchy Spirit can make your vision come to life. Contact us today to place an order or learn more about our products.

About the Artist

My name is Victoria. I am a homeschooling mom, U.S. Air Force Veteran, and M.S. Data Science Student. I love what I do. I started crocheting in 2014 as a way to cope with the struggle of returning from service after being medically retired.

I learned to crochet by following along with YouTube videos until I got the hang of it. Who knew I would fall in love with the art. It has since become more than a hobby, and now provides for my family.
